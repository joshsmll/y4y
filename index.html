About the Co-Founders

Joshua Small Culinary Director + Co-Organizer Josh is a 16 year old young chef and entrepreneur. With a passion for international cuisine, he's focused on creating trendy yet approachable dining experiences that blend high-end techniques as well as local ingredients. Josh has been fundraising for over 4 years. Out of the kitchen Josh is an avid runner and is half french. He's also an avid mixologist. He goes to school at the Lycée Français de New York, and spends his time in between NYC and the Catskills.
Alexander Morris Front of House Director + Co-Organizer Alexander is a dynamic 16 year old Youth event organizer and planner, with a passion for hospitality and crafting memorable and inclusive experiences. He has previously volunteered and served on the Youth Advisory Boards of various non profits including the Ronald McDonald House New York and the Billion Oyster Project. He currently attends Léman Prep High School, and is fluent in French and Spanish, as well as on the Varsity Basketball and Tennis teams.
